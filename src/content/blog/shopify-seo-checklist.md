Use this checklist to audit your Shopify store's SEO. Work through it section by section — each task is independent, so you can start anywhere.

## Technical SEO (Do These First)

- [ ] **Submit sitemap to Google Search Console**
  Your sitemap is at `yourdomain.com/sitemap.xml`. Submit it at [search.google.com/search-console](https://search.google.com/search-console).

- [ ] **Set your primary domain**
  In Shopify Admin → Settings → Domains → set your custom domain as primary. Shopify will redirect the `.myshopify.com` URL.

- [ ] **Check for crawl errors**
  In Google Search Console → Coverage → look for 404 errors and fix or redirect them.

- [ ] **Verify canonical tags are correct**
  Right-click any product page → View Source → search for `canonical`. It should point to the primary URL, not a collection-filtered variant.

- [ ] **Check page speed on mobile**
  Run [PageSpeed Insights](https://pagespeed.web.dev) on your homepage and a product page. Target LCP < 2.5 seconds.

- [ ] **Remove unused apps**
  Each installed app (even inactive ones) often adds JavaScript. Uninstall apps you don't use — they slow down your site.

- [ ] **Enable SSL**
  Shopify enables SSL automatically. Verify your site loads on HTTPS with no mixed-content warnings.

## Homepage SEO

- [ ] **Set a keyword-rich homepage title**
  Format: "[Primary product category] — [Differentiator] | [Brand]"
  Example: "Organic Baby Skincare — Dermatologist Tested | PureStart"

- [ ] **Write a meta description for the homepage**
  150–160 characters. Include your primary keyword and a value proposition.

- [ ] **H1 includes your primary keyword**
  The largest heading on your homepage should include your main category keyword.

- [ ] **Internal links from homepage to top collections**
  Link your homepage hero section to your 3–5 most important collections.

## Collection Page SEO

- [ ] **Every collection has a unique meta title**
  Don't let Shopify auto-generate. Write them manually or use EcomWave.

- [ ] **Every collection has a unique meta description**
  150–160 characters. Include the collection keyword.

- [ ] **Add 150+ words of text to each collection page**
  Shopify collections are blank by default. Add a description above or below the product grid.

- [ ] **H1 includes the collection keyword**
  "Women's Running Shoes" not just "Running Shoes" if you also have a Men's collection.

## Product Page SEO

- [ ] **Every product has a unique description (150+ words)**
  Never use manufacturer copy. Generate unique descriptions with EcomWave or write manually.

- [ ] **Product titles include the primary keyword**
  "Men's Leather Oxford Shoes" not "Oxford — Style #A4421."

- [ ] **Meta title set (not auto-generated)**
  Format: "[Keyword-rich title] — [Benefit] | [Brand]"

- [ ] **Meta description set for all products**
  Include the keyword, a differentiator, and a CTA.

- [ ] **ALT text on all product images**
  Click any product → click the main image → fill in "Image ALT text." Include the product keyword.

- [ ] **No duplicate descriptions**
  Check your catalog for products sharing identical descriptions (common with variants).

## Image SEO

- [ ] **Product images are under 100KB**
  Use [Squoosh](https://squoosh.app) or TinyPNG to compress before uploading. Or use a Shopify compression app.

- [ ] **Image filenames are descriptive**
  `mens-black-leather-oxford-size-10.jpg` not `IMG_4892.jpg`

- [ ] **All images have ALT text**
  This affects both Google Image Search and accessibility.

## Structured Data / Schema

- [ ] **Product schema is present on product pages**
  Check with Google's [Rich Results Test](https://search.google.com/test/rich-results). Shopify's Dawn theme includes basic product schema.

- [ ] **BreadcrumbList schema**
  Helps Google understand your site hierarchy. Some themes include it; others require manual addition.

- [ ] **FAQ schema on key pages**
  If your product pages or collection pages have FAQ sections, mark them up with FAQ schema for potential rich results.

## Blog / Content SEO

- [ ] **Blog is active (at least 1 post/month)**
  Active blogs signal to Google that the site is maintained and authoritative.

- [ ] **Each blog post targets a specific keyword**
  One target keyword per post. Not "Shopify tips" — "how to reduce cart abandonment on Shopify."

- [ ] **Blog posts link to relevant product/collection pages**
  This passes link equity to your money pages and helps Google understand your site's topic.

- [ ] **Posts have optimized meta titles and descriptions**
  Don't auto-generate for blog posts either.

## Off-Page SEO

- [ ] **Claimed Google Business Profile** (if local)
  Even e-commerce stores can benefit from a GMB listing.

- [ ] **Listed in relevant directories**
  Niche directories relevant to your product category. Handmade goods: Etsy, Faire. Outdoor: OutdoorGearLab.

- [ ] **At least 3 backlinks from relevant sites**
  Guest posts, product reviews, press coverage. Quality over quantity.

Running a profitable Shopify store doesn't mean spending hundreds on apps each month. You can build a solid tech stack using free plans — if you pick the right ones.

This guide covers the best free Shopify apps in 2025 across every major category: reviews, email, SEO, chat, upsells, loyalty, and shipping. Every app here was installed on real US-based stores and tested for speed impact, free plan limits, and actual usefulness.

Each app below was installed and tested on a [Shopify Basic plan](https://ecomwave.app/shopify-basic-plan-features/) store running the Dawn theme (OS 2.0). Results reflect real-world performance as of early 2025.

## Why Free Shopify Apps Still Matter in 2025

Meta ad costs for US e-commerce rose roughly 12% year-over-year heading into 2025 (Source: Revealbot, 2024). You're paying more just to get people to your store. So on-site conversion is now the most cost-effective way to grow revenue. Free apps help you convert more of that expensive traffic without adding to your monthly overhead.

The Shopify App Store lists over 8,000 apps. Competition among developers has pushed free tiers to be more generous than ever (Source: Shopify, 2024). One distinction matters here: **truly free apps** like Shopify Inbox have no paid tier at all. **Freemium apps** like Klaviyo offer a free plan with hard limits on contacts, orders, or features. Always read the pricing page before you install.

One warning. Free apps can conflict with each other — especially when multiple apps touch your cart, product page, or checkout. Install one app at a time. Run a speed test after each. Check for visual or functional conflicts before adding the next.

## How We Chose These Free Shopify Apps

Every app on this list meets a strict set of criteria. It had to carry a 4.5+ star rating from verified US merchants, receive at least one developer update within the past 90 days, and have no hidden charges on the free plan.

We tested each app on both a Shopify Basic ($39/month as of 2025) and a standard Shopify ($105/month as of 2025) plan store. OS 2.0 compatibility was required — that's Shopify's current theme architecture using app blocks and sections. If an app didn't work with Dawn or Sense without workarounds, it didn't make the cut.

We also measured **Core Web Vitals** impact using Google PageSpeed Insights before and after each install. Core Web Vitals are Google's page performance metrics. Any app that added more than 300ms to **Largest Contentful Paint (LCP)** — the time it takes for the largest visible element on a page to fully render — was flagged. We also checked data-privacy implications, especially around cookie consent and email collection practices relevant to US stores operating under CCPA.

## Best Free Shopify Apps for Email & SMS Marketing: Klaviyo for Automation, Shopify Email for Simplicity

Email still delivers the highest ROI of any marketing channel — $36 for every $1 spent on average (Source: Litmus, 2023). The right free email app helps you capture subscribers and automate follow-ups without touching your budget.

**Klaviyo** offers a free plan for up to 250 contacts and 500 email sends per month (as of 2025). What sets it apart is the depth of its **segmentation** — grouping subscribers by behavior, purchase history, or engagement level — and its pre-built automation flows for abandoned cart, welcome series, and win-back campaigns. It pulls your Shopify order data directly into the platform, so you can trigger emails based on real purchase behavior.

The DTC coffee brand Nguyen Coffee Supply built their early email program on Klaviyo's free tier before upgrading as their list grew. Merchants who set up even a basic three-email welcome series on Klaviyo's free plan often see measurable revenue within the first month. The automation runs 24/7 without manual effort.

The limitation: 250 contacts goes fast. Once you cross that threshold, Klaviyo's paid plans start at $20/month (as of 2025).

**Privy** is a good fit for early-stage stores. The free plan includes pop-up forms and basic email campaigns. It's simpler than Klaviyo, which makes it a reasonable starting point if you're new to email marketing. The tradeoff is significantly less sophisticated segmentation.

**Shopify Email** is built directly into your Shopify admin. Go to **Marketing > Campaigns > Create campaign > Shopify Email**. You get 10,000 free emails per month. It's the easiest option for beginners who want to send branded newsletters and promotions without learning a third-party tool.

**Omnisend** rounds out the list with a free plan that includes 500 emails per month plus web push notifications — a useful bonus for re-engaging visitors who don't subscribe to email.

**Pro tip:** Stack Shopify Email for broadcast campaigns — sale announcements, new arrivals — with Klaviyo for automated flows like abandoned cart and post-purchase sequences. Both free plans run at the same time without conflict.

## Best Free Shopify Apps for SEO: Plug In SEO and JSON-LD Cover the Essentials

Organic search is free traffic, but only if your store is set up to earn it. These apps handle the technical SEO tasks most store owners skip.

**Plug In SEO** scans your store for meta tag issues, broken links, and structured data problems on its free plan. It gives you a prioritized list of fixes — an SEO health check that runs inside your Shopify admin. You won't get bulk-fix capabilities on the free tier, but the diagnostic alone saves hours of manual auditing. Merchants who run Plug In SEO's free scan for the first time typically uncover 20–50 issues they didn't know existed, from duplicate meta descriptions to missing alt text.

**JSON-LD for SEO** by Ilana Davis adds **structured data markup** to your product pages automatically. This is code that helps search engines understand your page content. The free tier covers product schema, which helps Google display rich results — price, availability, reviews — in search listings. This works especially well paired with Judge.me. The two apps complement each other without conflicting schema.

**TinyIMG** offers a free plan for basic image compression. Large images are the number-one cause of slow Shopify stores, according to Shopify's own performance documentation (Source: Shopify Help Center, 2024). Compressing them improves Core Web Vitals scores and supports better Google rankings. The free tier handles a limited number of images per month, so stores with catalogs over 100 products will likely need to upgrade or compress in batches.

**Practical tip:** Most Shopify stores have dozens of product images with missing alt text — the descriptive text that helps search engines and screen readers understand what an image shows. You can fix alt text at scale using free bulk editor tools in the Shopify App Store. Search for "bulk image alt text" and look for apps with OS 2.0 support. ## Best Free Shopify Apps for Customer Support & Chat: Start with Shopify Inbox

Responding to a customer within five minutes makes you 21 times more likely to convert that interaction compared to responding after 30 minutes (Source: Harvard Business Review, 2011). Speed matters. A free chat app gets you there without hiring extra staff.

**Shopify Inbox** is 100% free with no paid tier. It connects your live chat with Facebook Messenger and Instagram DMs in a single interface. You can see a customer's cart contents during a conversation, which helps you answer product questions and close sales in real time. It's already in your Shopify admin under **Settings > Inbox** — you just activate it.

**Tidio** offers a free plan with live chat and a basic chatbot builder. A **chatbot** is an automated responder that handles common questions without human involvement. The free tier supports up to 50 live chat conversations per month. That makes it a solid pick for stores handling under 100 monthly support interactions. The chatbot can manage FAQs on shipping times and return policies automatically.

The limitation: once you exceed 50 conversations, Tidio's paid plans start at $29/month (as of 2025). That's a steep jump.

**Gorgias** is worth mentioning as an upgrade path, but it's not truly free — it offers a limited free trial. If your support volume outgrows Tidio's free plan, Gorgias is a strong next step with its helpdesk-style ticket management.

**Tip:** Create canned responses for your five most common questions. For US stores, these almost always include "Where is my order?", "What's your return policy?", "Do you ship to PO boxes?", "How long does shipping take?", and "Can I change my order?" Merchants who set up canned responses on day one often cut average reply time from several minutes to under 30 seconds.

## Free Shopify Apps to Avoid in 2025: Red Flags That Signal Trouble

Not all free apps deserve a spot in your store. Here's what to watch for.

**Abandoned apps** that haven't been updated in 12+ months are a red flag. Outdated apps create security vulnerabilities and often break after Shopify platform updates. Check the "Latest release" date on any app's listing before installing.

**Script-heavy apps** that inject excessive JavaScript deserve scrutiny. Some pop-up, countdown timer, and animation apps add 500KB+ of scripts that tank your Core Web Vitals. If your PageSpeed Insights score drops significantly after installing an app, remove it immediately.

**Intrusive mobile pop-ups** can hurt your search rankings. Google's interstitial penalty guidelines can push pages down in mobile search results if a pop-up covers the main content before the user interacts with the page (Source: Google Search Central, 2023). If you use a pop-up app, configure it to appear as a small banner — or trigger it only after the user scrolls or spends at least five seconds on the page.

**Fake reviews** on the app listing itself are a final warning sign. Steer clear of apps with suspiciously uniform five-star reviews or reviews that all appeared on the same date. Check the Shopify App Store review section and look for detailed, varied feedback from merchants describing specific use cases.

## How to Install and Manage Free Shopify Apps Safely

Install apps one at a time. Run a Google PageSpeed Insights test before and after each install so you can isolate the impact. If an app adds more than a second to your load time, it's probably not worth keeping.

Before clicking "Install," review the permissions the app requests. Some apps ask for access to your full customer list, order history, and store settings — far more than they need for their stated function. If a simple countdown timer app wants access to your customer data, that's a problem.

When you uninstall an app, check your theme files for leftover code. Many apps insert snippets into your theme.liquid or section files that don't get removed automatically. Go to **Online Store > Themes > Actions > Edit Code** and search for the app's name to find any remaining fragments.

Use Shopify's built-in speed report at **Online Store > Themes > View speed report** to monitor overall storefront performance. Keep a simple spreadsheet with each app's name, install date, purpose, and whether it passed your speed test. This makes quarterly audits painless and helps you quickly identify which app caused a problem if performance drops.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Are free Shopify apps really free with no hidden fees?

Most "free" apps use a freemium model — basic features cost nothing, but advanced features require a paid upgrade. Always check the pricing page before installing and read recent reviews for billing complaints. Truly free apps like Shopify Inbox and Shopify Email have no paid tier at all.

### How many free Shopify apps should I install?

Start with 5–8 core apps. Every app adds code to your store and can slow page load times. Only install what solves a specific problem you have right now. You can always add more later as your needs grow.

### Do free Shopify apps work with Shopify's latest themes in 2025?

Most reputable apps support Online Store 2.0 themes like Dawn and Sense. Check the app listing for OS 2.0 compatibility, or test on a duplicate theme first by going to **Online Store > Themes > Actions > Duplicate**. If you're choosing a theme, our [best free Shopify themes](/best-shopify-themes-free) guide covers OS 2.0 options.

### Which free Shopify app combination is best for increasing sales?

For most stores, a review app like Judge.me paired with an email capture tool like Klaviyo or Privy gives the fastest lift in conversion rate without spending anything. Reviews build trust at the product level. Email automation recovers abandoned carts and drives repeat purchases. Read our guide to the [Shopify Profit Margin Calculator](https://ecomwave.app/shopify-profit-margin-calculator/) for understanding which sales metrics matter most.

### Can I use free Shopify apps on the Basic Shopify plan?

Yes. Free apps work on all Shopify plans including Basic ($39/month as of 2025). Some apps restrict features based on your Shopify plan tier, but that's uncommon — check the app's FAQ or pricing page to confirm before installing.

### Will free apps slow down my Shopify store?

They can. Each app typically loads extra code, so monitor your Core Web Vitals before and after each installation.
